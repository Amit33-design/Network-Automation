FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max):
NetDesign AI turns plain English into complete network designs — instantly.

Describe your network in plain language and get:
✅ Professional network topology diagrams
✅ Complete device configurations (Cisco, Aruba, Juniper, Arista)
✅ IP addressing plans
✅ VLAN designs
✅ Bill of materials with cost estimates
✅ BGP and routing policies

DESIGNED FOR NETWORK ENGINEERS
Whether you're designing a campus network for 500 users, a data center with leaf-spine CLOS, or a GPU cluster for AI workloads — NetDesign AI handles the complexity so you can focus on the architecture.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Describe your network: "Design a campus network for 300 users with HA and wireless"
2. Review the topology diagram and device count
3. Generate configs for all devices in one click
4. Export or deploy directly to your devices

SUPPORTED USE CASES
• Campus networks (access/distribution/core 3-tier)
• Data center leaf-spine CLOS fabrics
• GPU / AI cluster networking (RoCEv2, non-blocking fat-tree)
• WAN / SD-WAN hub-and-spoke
• Multi-site enterprise networks

SUPPORTED VENDORS
Cisco IOS, Cisco NX-OS, Aruba AOS-CX, Juniper Junos, Arista EOS
